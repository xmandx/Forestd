Our mission

At Forestd, we are committed to promoting awareness and preserving forests around the world. Our mission is to provide accurate, educational and inspiring information about forests, highlighting their vital importance for the ecological balance, biodiversity and sustainability of our planet.

We believe that every tree, every forest ecosystem, is a fundamental piece in the complex puzzle of life on Earth. Therefore, we are dedicated to sharing knowledge about different types of forests, their unique characteristics and the challenges faced in their conservation.

About us

At Forestd, we believe that forests are the lungs of our planet, essential for the health of our environment and the well-being of all life forms. Our website was born out of a passion for these vital ecosystems and a desire to share knowledge and inspire action for their preservation. Our team is made up of biologists, conservationists, educators and forest enthusiasts, united by a common goal: to promote awareness about the importance of forests and encourage sustainable practices. Through informative articles, educational videos, photo galleries and interactive features, we seek to provide an enriching and inspiring experience for anyone interested in learning more about our planet's forests. Join us on this journey of discovery, learning and protecting our precious forests.

Our heart, our life.
